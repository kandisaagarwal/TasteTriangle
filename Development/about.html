About Us

Taste Triangle

The working professionals in our country who are the backbone of our country's economy. They do not get good healthy homemade food as they don't have much time to cook for themselves. The tiffin providers are very small scale they lack in terms of proper education and funds and are enable to provide delivery to their customers. Hence they end up quitting their business. Hence through our platform these very small scale Tiffin providers who work from their home and provide homemade food. Our motive is to provide management solution as well as financial support to these small scale Tiffin providers and make it easier for the working professionals to order Tiffin online and have healthy food daily.
